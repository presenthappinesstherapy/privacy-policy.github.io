Privacy Policy: Present Happiness Therapy

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we handle and store your personal information using the Present Happiness Therapy app.

Data Collection and Use

We do not collect any personal data through our app. All data you enter is stored only on your device and is not sent or stored on our servers.

Our Use of AsyncStorage

The app uses AsyncStorage to store your inputted data, meaning all data is stored only on your device. This data can be deleted by uninstalling the app from your device.

Data Sharing

Since we do not collect or store your data on our servers, it will not be shared with third parties.

Data Deletion

If you wish to delete your data, simply uninstall our app from your device. This will automatically delete all data stored in the app.

Changes to Our Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted in this privacy policy.
